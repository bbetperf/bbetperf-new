Mesoki.

Project. Nov 23.

Established a minimalist stationery brand that seamlessly blends functional design and engaging digital storytelling.

Overview.

Most stationery brands exist entirely in the physical world. Mesoki was designed to live in both — products that look as considered on a desktop as they do in a photograph. The brand identity, packaging, and digital presence were designed as a single system rather than separate deliverables.

Details.

Identity.
Wordmark

A custom wordmark built from geometric forms — clean enough to emboss, distinctive enough to recognize at thumbnail scale.

Packaging.
Print

Uncoated paper stock, minimal ink coverage, and deliberate white space. The packaging feels as considered as the products inside.

Digital.
E-commerce

The online store extends the brand's visual language — editorial photography, sparse layouts, and copy that reads like a letter rather than a product description.
