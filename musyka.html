Musyka.

Project. Nov 24.

Designed a vibrant digital music experience tailored specifically to the dynamic preferences of new generation.

Overview.

Streaming platforms have converged on identical interfaces. Musyka was an exploration of what music discovery looks like when it's designed for energy — for listeners who find music through short-form video, shared playlists, and social signals rather than editorial curation.

Details.

Discovery.
Social-first

Tracks surface through a friends-feed, trending clips, and shared moments — not an algorithm's recommendation.

Player.
Immersive

The now-playing screen fills the display with responsive album art and animated waveforms. Minimal chrome, maximum focus on the music.

Visual Language.
Dynamic

Color schemes adapt to the album artwork using a dominant-hue extraction algorithm — every track has its own palette.
